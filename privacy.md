# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 2, 2026*

## Introduction

Welcome to TikFlow.

TikFlow respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we may collect information that you authorize through the official TikTok APIs, including:

* Basic profile information
* Public account information
* Access and refresh tokens (when required for authentication)
* Information necessary to provide the requested features

We do not collect any information beyond the permissions granted by the user.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

* Authenticate your account
* Display your TikTok profile information
* Provide features requested by you
* Improve the application
* Ensure security and reliability

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and reasonable measures are taken to protect it against unauthorized access.

## Data Sharing

TikFlow does not sell or rent your personal information.

Information is only shared when required by law or when necessary to provide the requested service.

## Third-Party Services

TikFlow uses official TikTok APIs for authentication and data access.

## Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting the application administrator.

## Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any updates.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the application administrator.
